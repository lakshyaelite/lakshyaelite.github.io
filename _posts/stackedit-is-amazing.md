I know it's a pain in the back to write and publish Jekyll blogs because I am too a owner of a Jekyll blog. Now, I know that writing in markdown is easy and all of that. But, you see, StackEdit has a ton of features like directly posting to your GitHub Jekyll blog.

## What is StackEdit?

[StackEdit](https://stackedit.io) is a free and open-source markdown writing web app. It just makes writing so much easier! I don't need to go to Google or any cheatsheet every 10 seconds just to know how to make something **bold** in markdown, it's all right there!

## Why even care?

StackEdit is not just good for writing markdown files, it can export them to HTML code too! And if you for some reason use Google's Blogger (yuck), then you can directly publish your files to it too.

I think overall this app looks amazing! From writing blogs to making plain websites, it is great. Don't worry about the "Written with StackEdit" text at the end, I am just keeping it for showoff.
