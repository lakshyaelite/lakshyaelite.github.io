There are tons and tons of laptop accessories in the market, and no matter what a random Shorts YouTuber says about the accessories you need, here are some laptop accessories you might actually want to buy and find useful.

***NOTE:** The products I have recommended below are not sponsored. I DO NOT accept sponsorships at the moment.*

## 1. Laptop Stand
This one I think is a must if you are planning to use your laptop for a long time. A laptop stand allows air to pass more easily through your laptop's vents, making it cooler. And when your laptop gets cooler, it runs faster. Many laptop stands are foldable, allowing portability, and some are customisable with adding name and other things. Overall, I think this is a must to keep you laptop healthy.

**My pick:** Portronics My Buddy K-Lite (Blue)

## 2. Mouse
Yes, a mouse. Why? Because if you work for a long period of time on your laptop, getting a mouse can increase your speed and productivity. Tough, I do want to point out that you should go with a bit mid ranger type of mouse, as cheap mice (around ₹150) often get problems like the scroll wheel not scrolling properly in months. So, take your time and do your research and buy the best mouse for you.

**My pick:** Portronics Toad II

## 3. Mouse pad (not needed for all)
This one's optional for people who work on tables with wooden or other rough surfaces, but for those who are like me who work on their dining table and it's surface is glass or marble or anything smooth, you might want to consider this. Trust me, I never thought I need a mouse pad until I got one for myself. You suddenly feel more grip on you mouse and freedom to move it. If you buy one, please tape it's edges or it will start to peel off.

**My pick:** Amazon Basics
